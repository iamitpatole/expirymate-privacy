# Account Deletion Instructions for ExpiryMate

If you wish to delete your ExpiryMate account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below:

## How to Request Account Deletion

1. Send an email to our support team at:
   
   **expirymate.support@gmail.com**

2. Use the subject line: `Account Deletion Request`

3. In the body of your email, please include:
   - The email address associated with your ExpiryMate account
   - A brief statement confirming you want your account and all data deleted

   **Example:**
   > Hello,
   >
   > I would like to permanently delete my ExpiryMate account and all associated data. My account email is: your@email.com
   >
   > Thank you.

4. Our team will process your request and confirm once your account has been deleted. This may take up to 7 days.

## Important Notes
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
- All your data, including items, groups, and history, will be removed from our servers.
- If you have any questions, please contact us at the support email above.
